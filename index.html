About Me

My name is Hanna Kilpatrick, but I go by Anya. I’m from Ukraine, and I moved to the United States in 2016. I have a bachelors in Philology, the study of languages. When I moved to America, I realized that I needed to start my career from the ground up, and that’s when I discovered the Full Stack Web Development Program at University of Richmond. I had no experience in programming, but my love for creativity and creating things told me that this was exactly what I wanted to do.

The classes started in January, and I continue to learn new things every day. I’m excited to keep progressing in this field, and I’m eager to make a career out of it.
